Aviation Accident Data
                  

Target Audience
Head of Aviation Division
Non-technical business leader


Business Problem
The company does not have aviation experience on the safety side of things.
Poor aircraft selection might result in financial losses because it will involve alot of maintenance and high insurance cost.
Alao it can lead to reputational damage




Our method: The process
Historical aviation accident data from 1962-2023 analyzed
Focused on:
Aircraft manufacturers
Aircraft models
Injury severity and fatalities
Used descriptive analytics and clear visualizations to assess risk



Findings
Some aircraft manufacturers have kept fatality counts per year with a great consistency.
Certain aircraft models involved in accidents at a higher rate
Most accidents involves injuries only so selection can occur based on risk

Results
Provides data driven results that would guide aircraft acquisition.
It reduces safety,financial and operational risks. Lays the ground for future aviation analytics
